NZSL Dictionary Official App

New Zealand Sign Language is one of the official languages of New Zealand. It became the third official language in April 2006 and has an estimated 24,000 speakers.

The Deaf Studies Resarch Unit, Victoria University of Wellington publishes The Online Dictionary of New Zealand Sign Language under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License.

This app was originally developed and generously gifted to the DSRU by Greg Hewgill. It is maintained by Rabid Technologies.
